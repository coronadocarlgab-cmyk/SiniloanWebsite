Laguna, Philippines

Waterfall sanctuary, friendly town energy

Siniloan sits between the Sierra Madre and Laguna de Bay. Trek Mt. Romelo to Buruwisan Falls, learn local traditions, and wind down at a cozy farm stay.

Mt. Romelo Treks

Beginner-friendly trails leading to multiple falls and river pools. Go with a local guide for safety and to keep trails clean. Source: community hiking guides.
