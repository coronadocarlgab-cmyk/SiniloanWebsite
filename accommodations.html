PasEla Farm

PasEla Farm, located at Macatad Ecotourism Site Km.3, Siniloan, Laguna , is an eco-agricultural tourism destination that is promoting sustainable farming and rural tourism. Originally, PasEla Farm established as a small, family-owned agricultural land which has cultivated vegetables, herbs, and fruit-bearing trees. Over the years, it has evolved into a venue where tourists can immerse themselves in farm life, offering various activities that brings the people closer to nature.
