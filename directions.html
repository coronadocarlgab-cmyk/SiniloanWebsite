Directions

From Metro Manila (public)

  • Bus or van to Famy/Siniloan (via Rizal or via SLEX to Laguna hub), then tricycle to town or jump‑off.
  • Allow 3–4 hours depending on traffic and weather.

By private vehicle

  • Via Rizal lake road or SLEX–Calamba–Pagsanjan corridor, then east to Siniloan.
  • Check road conditions during rainy months.
