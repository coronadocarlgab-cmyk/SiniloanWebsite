Ilog Putol Bridge

Ilog Putol Bridge is a man-made bridge located in the Municipality of Siniloan, Laguna, within the Calabarzon region of the Philippines. It spansnover part of the Siniloan (Romelo) River and is situated near attractions like the Ilog Putol Sunset View park and I Heart Sulib park.

Panguil River Eco Park

Nestled along the Panguil River a tributary of Laguna de Bay the eco-park spans roughly 12 to 14 hectares at the foot of the Sierra Madre mountain range. Season & Safety

Best months: December to May. Trails get muddy in peak rains. Wear proper footwear; hire a guide if new to the area.
