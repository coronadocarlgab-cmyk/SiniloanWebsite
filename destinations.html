Destinations

Always check trail conditions with local authorities or guides. Pack out trash. Respect private land.

Buruwisan Falls

Iconic 50–60 m drop along Mt. Romelo routes, popular with hikers and canyoning groups. Clear pools during dry months.

Romelo River

Feeds multiple falls on the Mt. Romelo side before reaching Laguna de Bay. Good for photos, not for littering.

Town Landmarks

Stop by the town plaza and parish church before or after treks. Great for quick cultural context and snacks.

Season & Safety

Best months: December to May. Trails get muddy in peak rains. Wear proper footwear; hire a guide if new to the area.
