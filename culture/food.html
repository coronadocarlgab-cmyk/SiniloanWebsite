Native Foods

Siniloan, Laguna, offers a variety of native delicacies, with Sinukmani, a sweet sticky rice cake, and Uraro, a type of cookie made from arrowroot flour, being particularly well-known. Other popular treats include Buko pie, Kesong puti (white cheese), and various cassava-based dishes.

Buko Pie

A custard pie filled with tender young coconut meat, creating a creamy and sweet dessert experience. This classic Filipino treat is perfect for any occasion and showcases local coconut flavors.
